The Bloom Box

For CAPS, we competed in the SM Wilson Centennial Idea Competition. Our prompt for the competition was to make a project that will encourage students from k-12 to get involved in STEAM. I was the project leader for my group, and we created an activity for students in 8-12th grade where they will build garden boxes and decorate them to create a story. We ended up winning a $1,000 award from this project.

BizFitz

Another project we did for CAPS was a competition called "Battle of the Saints". Our prompt was to make a project/business that solves an issue. Our business was called BizFitz and it is an online business where we take thrifted clothings and make it into affordable and stylish business clothing.
